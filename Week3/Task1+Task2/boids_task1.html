Simulation Controls

Mathematical Model

Separation

Steers the boid away from local flockmates to prevent collisions. The force is the sum of vectors pointing away from neighbors, weighted inversely by distance to prioritize immediate threats.

F_sep = Σ (p_i - p_j) / ||p_i - p_j||²

Alignment

Steers the boid towards the average heading (velocity) of its local flockmates, keeping the flock moving in a unified direction.

F_ali = (Average Velocity) - v_i

Cohesion

Steers the boid towards the average position (center of mass) of its local flockmates, keeping the group clumped together.

F_coh = (Average Position) - p_i

Perception Radius

Defines the local neighborhood space. For any given boid, only other boids that fall within this Euclidean distance are factored into the calculations above.
